Phrase App

User Stories
Main
1. As a user, I want to be able to search a phrase
2. As a user, I want to the foreign phrase spoken to me so I can hear the pronunciation
3. As a user, I want to see related phrases based on situations (introductions, eating, transpotation)
5. As a user, I want to be able to add phrases to my phrasebook
6. As a user, I want to be able to favorite phrases
7. As a user, I want to view previously searched phrases

Optional
1. As a user, I want the app to detect what language I am using
2. As a user, I want to dissect a sentence (is a word a noun or a verb | color coded).
